    • About Our Farm

    It started with five acres of land located just east of the Hudson River in Kinderhook, New York. In 1987, the Hoyt brothers, Harold and Hoss, left city life in Brooklyn behind to start working and making a living from the land.

    Over the past three decades the Hoyt brothers have turned their humble vegetable farm into a 100 acre farm specialzing in hundreds of varieties of organic fruits, vegetables, and livestock, including chickens, pigs, and goats.

    Our mission and promise to you at Hoyt Farms is to grow the highest quality products for our neighbors.




    Our Field

    At Hoyt Farms, we grow hundreds of varieties of different fruits and vegetables. We are committed to growing organic, specialty, and heirloom varieties that thrive here in the Northeast. We grow many exotic colors of traditional vegetables that we grow that will give you a great mix of vitamins and phytonutrients. Our vegetables taste different than those you will find in the supermarket and even those that you purchase locally from conventional growers. As a certified organic farm, we pay special attention to the quality and health of our soil and water which directly affects the health, taste, and beauty of our produce.

    Text adapted from Sang Lee Farms.





    Our Barn

    The barn is home to a mix of lively animals that help us produce a variety of products for our neighbors and friends to enjoy. Stop by the Farm Stand Wednesday through Sunday from 8am to 6pm to bring the farm to your kitchen. We have your fresh eggs, heritage pork, goat's milk and cheeses.


    Our People

    We are the staff of Hoyt Farms. We work in the field, in the office, the farmstand, and at the farmer's markets. We come from the USA, Chile, Ecuador, Germany, and Vietnam. We love working on Hoyt Farms and growing the finest food for you.
